I am a lecturer at School of Software Engineering, Beihang University. Before I joined Beihang, I received my Ph.D. degree at the Advanced Multimedia Research Laboratory in the University of Wollongong, Australia, under the supervision of A/Prof. Wanqing Li and Prof. Philip Ogunbona. My research is on computer vision and machine learning, with the special interests in transfer learning, domain adaptation, and other related topics.
